# Event Rules and Regulations

## Individual Event Rules

### General Rules
1. **Single Event Participation**: Each student can participate in multiple individual events unless specified otherwise by the event coordinator.
2. **Registration Deadline**: All registrations must be completed before the specified deadline. Late registrations will not be accepted.
3. **Identity Verification**: Participants must carry their college ID card and present it when requested during the event.
4. **Punctuality**: Participants must report to the venue at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time. Late arrivals may be disqualified.

### Conduct Rules
5. **Fair Play**: Any form of cheating, plagiarism, or unfair means will result in immediate disqualification.
6. **Respectful Behavior**: Participants must maintain decorum and show respect to fellow participants, judges, and organizers. Misbehavior may lead to disqualification.
7. **Property Respect**: Any damage to college property or equipment will result in penalties and potential disqualification.
8. **Substance Policy**: Consumption of alcohol, drugs, or any prohibited substances is strictly forbidden on college premises during the event.

### Technical Rules
9. **Equipment Responsibility**: Participants are responsible for their own equipment unless otherwise specified. The organizing committee is not liable for any loss or damage.
10. **Rules Compliance**: All participants must adhere to the specific rules of the event as communicated by the coordinators.
11. **Judge's Decision**: The decision of judges and coordinators is final and binding. Participants must accept the verdict gracefully.

### Disciplinary Actions
12. **Immediate Disqualification**: Fighting, violence, or aggressive behavior will result in instant disqualification and potential disciplinary action.
13. **Warning System**: Participants may receive warnings for minor rule violations. Three warnings will result in disqualification.

## Team/Group Event Rules

### Team Formation & Registration
1. **Team Size Compliance**: Teams must adhere to the minimum and maximum team size requirements specified for each event.
2. **Single Team Participation**: Each student can participate in only ONE team per group event. Participation in multiple teams for the same event is prohibited.
3. **Team Registration**: All team members must be registered before the deadline. Incomplete team registrations will not be accepted.
4. **Team Leader Responsibility**: Each team must designate a team leader who will be the primary point of contact and responsible for coordinating with organizers.

### Member Eligibility
5. **College Students Only**: All team members must be currently enrolled students of the college with valid student IDs.
6. **Cross-Department Teams**: Unless specified otherwise, teams can consist of members from different departments and years.
7. **No Substitutions**: Team member substitutions after registration are not allowed except in case of medical emergencies (requires prior approval with documentation).

### Conduct & Fair Play
8. **Team Collaboration**: All team members must participate actively. Proxy participation or impersonation is strictly prohibited and will lead to team disqualification.
9. **Fair Competition**: Any form of cheating, plagiarism, sabotage of other teams, or use of unfair means will result in immediate disqualification of the entire team.
10. **Respectful Behavior**: Teams must maintain decorum and show respect to fellow teams, judges, and organizers. Misbehavior by any team member may lead to entire team disqualification.

### Technical & Operational Rules
11. **Punctuality**: The entire team must report to the venue at least 20 minutes before the scheduled start time. Late arrival of any team member may result in team disqualification.
12. **Equipment & Resources**: Teams are responsible for their own equipment unless otherwise specified. The organizing committee is not liable for any loss or damage.
13. **Rules Compliance**: All team members must adhere to the specific rules of the event as communicated by the coordinators.

### Disciplinary Actions
14. **Zero Tolerance for Violence**: Fighting, violence, or aggressive behavior by any team member will result in immediate disqualification of the entire team and potential disciplinary action.
15. **Collective Responsibility**: All team members are collectively responsible for the conduct of their team. Violations by one member may affect the entire team.
16. **Judge's Authority**: The decision of judges and coordinators is final and binding. Teams must accept the verdict gracefully.

### Payment & Verification
17. **Team Payment**: One registration fee per team (not per member). Payment must be verified for all team members to participate.
18. **Proof of Registration**: Teams must carry their registration confirmation and be ready to present it when requested.

## Common Rules for All Events

### Code of Conduct
- **Professional Behavior**: Maintain professional and courteous behavior at all times.
- **Substance-Free Environment**: Consumption of alcohol, drugs, or prohibited substances is strictly forbidden.
- **Property Care**: Respect and care for college property, equipment, and facilities.

### Safety & Security
- **Emergency Protocols**: Follow all safety instructions and emergency protocols announced by organizers.
- **Authorized Areas Only**: Participants must remain in designated areas and not enter restricted zones.

### Communication
- **Official Announcements**: Participants must stay updated with official announcements through designated channels.
- **Coordinator Contact**: Report any issues or emergencies immediately to event coordinators.

### Final Authority
- **Organizing Committee**: The event organizing committee reserves the right to modify rules, cancel events, or take decisions in unforeseen circumstances.
- **Disputes**: Any disputes or clarifications should be addressed to the organizing committee whose decision will be final.
